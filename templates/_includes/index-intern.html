Design Métier

This is a beta version of SNCF's Bootstrap based on original Twitter's Bootstrap (it's a fork !).

You are a designer and / or developer and you want to create an internal interface, dedicated to the production, and with no purpose of communication ? You are in the right place !

Otherwise, if you want to make an interface for internal communication purpose or just an interface for our customers, you can go to the Bootstrap Communication.

In recent years, many internal applications created have unfortunately resulted in websites or applications far from the graphic chart, even totally off the chart. Similarly, many developers have sometimes spent or wasting time coding already existing components. This Bootstrap is for you, and especially with you ! How to contribute ?

For more informations, contact us at design.fab@sncf.fr, we'd love hear about your project and how we could help you !
